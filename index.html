Welcome to the Two-player Dragon-Ball Game by Parth Agarwal.

Controls:
Move Goku (1st character) left and right with a and d, press w to jump and press space-bar to shoot.
Move Beerus (2nd character) left and right with arrow keys, press up-arrow to jump and ctrl to shoot.
